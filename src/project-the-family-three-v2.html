The Family Three App

Study Case

The Family App helps bring the tough conversations together. Your love ones affected by Alzheimer's are headed through a difficult time but it doesn’t have to be. The Family platform allows users to upload their family photos and use real memories in the treatment of the patient. Personalized care help provide better conversations and better results in the treatment of Alzheimers. The family app is designed to bring families together, connect with their caretakers, and work to promote a healthy relationship with their loved ones.

Families can share photos, save important moments, and use personalized content with our cognitive enhancement program. The caregiver can develop learning programs to pinpoint areas of improvement while learning more about the patients they service care.

Discover

Discovery of Problem

Hospitals are impacted with an overload of patients that need dedicated service and treatments. The time it takes to develop a personalized plan is daunting for the doctor to administer while the patient’s family begins to feel as if the treatments are not enough to assist their loved one. It’s a sensitive time as the memory fades away from the patient but what needs to happen is better more detailed care that doesn’t overload the already overworked doctors.

Conclusions

After testing with several different personas, I found that patients responded better with activities built around family and friends. They surveyed highest when viewing photos that involved loved ones and real memories then when they were presented with general photos.

The family member surveyed that they felt more involved in the care of their loved ones. With increased awareness of all the activities, the family members enjoyed collecting photos and organizing them in a way to improve treatment for their loved ones. When asked about the process, the family members said that trust in the doctors plan of action was of highest satisfactory.
