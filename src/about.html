Greyci Svendsen
Who I am

I am Danish/Peruvian student that came to America to do my master program.

My passion is for technology and design. I enjoy the development process of great user experiences by designing, creating and organizing the interfaces people interact with on a daily basis. I love being creative and adding a little flare to simple designs.

My career goal is to work for a fast-paced technology company using my skills to showcase user centric design.
I like to find unique solutions that help people improve their daily life and have chosen project topics aligned with daily activity. It’s difficult to gain regular attention from users and great user design invites the user to return with excitement.
