Restaurant ETTAN

Study Case

Ettan is a new bay area restaurant that opened in 2020 with the concept of high end Indian cuisine creating a unique confluence of the Indian spice filled palette with California fresh ingredients. The goal is to encourage people to experience the richness and depth of Indian flavors in a vibrant and healthy manner.

My task is re-branding Ettan as a restaurant.

Naming & Visual Identity
Building the brand

I chose to create a logo with the golden flower pattern as the major representation.
This is represents a natural and casual feeling.

Mission: I have to create a authentic Indian style them that promoted a high end restaurant experience with cultural influences.

Vision: My visions was to capture icons and imagery found in Indian culture while still trying to reflect a American fusion. I wanted to create a fun and exciting experience that represented quality and unique dining experiences.
