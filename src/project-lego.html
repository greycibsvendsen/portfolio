Marketing design, branding

LEGO
Advertising for LEGO

Study Case

LEGO has digitized its building guides connecting their projects to the upcoming generations. A more immersive and easier way to use LEGO’S.

Build faster, easier, and better than ever before!

The next generation is increasingly involved with their devices. LEGOs app will resonate with the current and future generations while improve the customer experience.. Their next steps are marketing their new app.

Project Type

Marketing Design, Branding

Role

Branding Strategy, Marketing, and Design.

Timeline

4 weeks

Tools

AI Illustrator, PS Photoshop, ID Indesign

Advertising Focus

1. To parent

2. To College Students

3. To Elementary School

Mission

Inspire the builders
of tomorrow

Social media
advertising

Advertising on social media. The target audience is parents playing with their children with LEGO. The ad demonstrates how they can use the app on their tablet to enhance their building adventure. Parents can help their children assisted by LEGO’s digital support.

Digital table
advertising

This ad targets architect college students by taking their designs to a fun and creative medium of building their work with Lego! LEGO’s Build app helps students interact with their designs within a 3D model and provides useful information about the size and scope of their project.

Poster

This ad’s target audience kids in elementary school.
This advertisement will be placed around schools to create awareness of legos new
Build app, a smarter and more creative way to interact with LEGO.
